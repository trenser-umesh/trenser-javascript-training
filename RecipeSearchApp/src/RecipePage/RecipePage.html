Recipe Tips

• Add fresh herbs toward the end of cooking. This helps their flavor come through and prevents discoloring.

• Gather all the tools you’ll need, like bowls, cutting boards and utensils before you get started, dropping them in their respective prep zones.

• Season and taste as you cook: Season and taste your food while you're cooking.
